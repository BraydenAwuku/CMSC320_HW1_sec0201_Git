
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git makes it easy for people to experiment and go back if needed. It 
tracks changes over time and lets several people work safely on the same
project.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example.

Answer: One way data science can be applied is in e-commerce. It suggests 
products to customers based on their previous browsing and buying 
behavior. 

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches with a new commit that keeps their 
history. Rebasing puts your branch on top of another branch to create a 
new, cleaner linear history.
